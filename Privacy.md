## What we collect and why
- **Name** — collected via Sign in with Apple; used as your display name to friends.
- **User ID (Firebase UID)** — used to attribute your habits and check-ins to you.
- **Device push token (FCM)** — used to send you reminders you explicitly enable.
- **Habits, check-ins, challenges, friend relationships** — the product itself.
- **Other users' display names and content (challenge titles, emoji)** — shown to you in-app.

We do **not** use advertising, we do **not** track you across apps, and we do **not** sell data.

## Where data lives
Google Firebase (Firestore + Auth + Cloud Messaging), hosted on Google Cloud. Google acts as our data processor under a standard Data Processing Addendum.

## How long we keep it
Account data is kept until you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account). Firestore deletion runs within minutes; backups age out within 30 days.
